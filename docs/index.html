Coerthas "Dank"

A stout brewed in the Coerthan highland regions in various cellar breweries, hiding away from pious eyes.

Despite societal reform, the art of brewing is still frowned upon within the Ishgard city proper resulting in significant variation in quality.

Sharishja Qhakyisha

This is something the ananta sell to travelers. We're not exactly sure what's in it, but it tastes a bit like ale and... herbs?

Getting this stuff in larger quantities was a pain in the rear - you better like it.

Thirteenth Batch

Buscarron of Gridania brews a special wheat beer primarily for off-duty Woodwailers. Each season the recipe is changed to prevent locals from becoming too fond of it.

Dai Coo Gain

East Aldenard Trading Company rarely imports beverages, but prefers to commission localized copycat variant products instead. This transparent liquor mimics the taste of an age-old Hingan dinner aperitive.

Adhibari Bhujamga

A very strong Hannish herb liquor infused with a thavnarian snake. Popular with tourists looking for a memorable night.
